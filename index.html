About Us

Born & Braid serves up homemade challah baked to order by Ioana Friedman in the heart of South Orange, NJ. Small batch, fresh ingredients, no preservatives.

Text (973) 378-0880 to order by Wednesday. Pick up or $1 local delivery in the SOMA area Friday by 5pm.

Get your challah

Born & Braid challah is delivered fresh and made with wheat flour, water, wildflower honey, sunflower oil, fresh eggs, yeast, sugar, and salt. Choose from four different flavors: Plain, Sesame, Poppy, Everything.
