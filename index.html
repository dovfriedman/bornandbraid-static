Small Batch

Born & Braid serves hand-crafted, homemade challah baked to order by Ioana Friedman in the heart of South Orange, NJ. Small batch, fresh ingredients, no preservatives. We serve a core group of member families, beyond which we offer limited quantities for purchase every week. First-come, first-served, while supplies last.

How to Order

Weekly orders open 9 AM Monday. Text (973) 378-0880 to place your order. Word is spreading faster than our ovens can produce, so text early because we sell out fast, especially for Friday orders. We will accept your order and provide Venmo details. Your order will be considered confirmed once we receive payment.

Pick up Wednesday or Friday by 5 PM in South Orange. Local delivery is available on Friday in the SOMA area for $3. Please note, while we aim to bring you beautiful challah, we are not liable for any change or damage to the product after delivery, be it due to weather or other unforeseen events.

Due to high demand, order inquiries received after we sell out will NOT roll over into the following week.

Ingredient Info

We make our challah exclusively with King Arthur flour and fresh ingredients, no preservatives. Born & Braid challah contains wheat flour, water, wildflower honey, sunflower oil, fresh eggs, yeast, salt, and all-natural food colors in our specialty colorful loaves. We bake in a kitchen that also processes nuts.

We do not offer gluten-free, vegan, or whole wheat options at this time.

We use kosher ingredients, but do not have a hechsher or operate under rabbinical supervision. All of our challah loaves and rolls are parve, save for our sweets, which contain dairy.
