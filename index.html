Small Batch

Born & Braid serves hand-crafted, homemade challah baked to order by Ioana Friedman in the heart of South Orange, NJ. Small batch, fresh ingredients, no preservatives. We serve a core group of member families, beyond which we offer limited quantities for purchase every week. First-come, first-served, while supplies last.

How to Order

The word is spreading faster than our ovens can produce, so order early because we sell out fast. We will confirm your order and provide Venmo details. Due to high demand, order inquiries received after we sell out will NOT roll over into the following week.

Ingredient Info

We proudly make our challah exclusively with King Arthur Flour and fresh ingredients, no preservatives. Born & Braid challah contains wheat flour, water, wildflower honey, sunflower oil, fresh eggs, yeast, and salt.

We do not offer vegan, whole wheat, or gluten-free options at this time.

Even though we use kosher ingredients, we are not a certified kosher brand. All of our challah loaves and rolls are parve (dairy-free) unless, of course, you're ordering one of our delicious cinnamon buns, which contain butter.
